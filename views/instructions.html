Controls:

  • WASD or arrow keys = Move player
  • WASD or arrow keys + shift = Place or remove tile
  • 0-9 keys = Perform battle action (after binding)

Explanation of stats:

  • Level determines maximum health and base attack strength.
  • Experience points (XP) are required to level up and learn actions.
  • Health points (HP) are required to survive.
  • Energy points (EP) are required to perform actions.
  • Damage points (DP) determine the scaling factor for attack strength.
  • Gold is rewarded upon winning battles.
  • Score is the cumulative amount of XP collected.
